Protein Sequencer

Reads fasta format of Human Metabolome Database, creates objects to store each specific sequence of amino acids (protein sequence) and collects them in an array list. It then searches for specific sub sequences, counts occurrences of specific subsequences and writes the desired protein information back in fasta form.
